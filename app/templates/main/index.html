About Mazingira Yetu Initiative

Mazingira Yetu Initiative is a Kenyan non-profit organization dedicated to promoting sustainable waste management practices. Our vision is to transform Kenya into a model of environmental responsibility and cleanliness within a decade.

Through community engagement, innovative technology, and collaboration, we aim to eliminate waste-related pollution and create a healthier environment for all Kenyans.

Ripoti Taka Program

Our flagship Ripoti Taka Program empowers individuals to report and address waste-related issues. By utilizing modern technology, gamification, and community involvement, we're revolutionizing waste management.

Learn More

How the Ripoti Taka Program Works

Our Ripoti Taka Program operates through a simple process:

  1. Citizens report waste-related issues using our user-friendly web or mobile app.
  2. Our team receives the reports and coordinates responses with stakeholders.
  3. Real-time updates are provided to users as issues are resolved.
  4. Engage with the community through gamification and rewards.
